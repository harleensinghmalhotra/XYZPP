# Homepage — Products

**Appears on the site:** homepage → "What We Print" category cards (shown as
transparent cut-outs that sit on the cream cards with a soft shadow).

**How to swap:** replace a file below with your own, keeping the **exact same
filename and extension**. Best source = a book/product grouping shot on a **plain
white background** — the site auto-removes a clean white background and adds the
silhouette shadow. (A photo on a coloured or grey studio background will show that
background as a box on the card.) Push to deploy.

# Hero typing-book sound

## Active: `keyboard-typing.wav`

A ~6.6s continuous mechanical-keyboard typing recording, played as a **looped
bed** that runs while characters are actively typed and pauses shortly after
(see [`src/lib/typingSound.js`](../../../../src/lib/typingSound.js)), OFF by
default. Confirm redistribution rights before shipping to production.

## Retired: `key-click-1.wav`, `key-click-2.wav`, `key-click-3.wav`

Procedurally synthesised click sounds (a decaying filtered-noise transient plus
a short low "thock" body). They contain no third-party audio and are released as
**CC0 / public domain** — no attribution required. No longer used.

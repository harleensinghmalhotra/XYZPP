## Style family (siblings)

All four are the **`clay`** style at the **`dynamic`** camera angle — the same
material and lighting, so they read as a set. The soft neutral clay was chosen
over the vivid gradient/color styles because it sits closest to Ekta's cream +
navy palette and duotones cleanly.

CDN pattern: `https://3dicons.sgp1.cdn.digitaloceanspaces.com/v1/dynamic/clay/{slug}-dynamic-clay.png`

| File in this folder | 3dicons slug | Stat |
|---|---|---|
| `books.png` | `notebook` | 400M+ Books Delivered |
| `countries.png` | `sphere` | 25+ Countries Across 4 Continents |
| `containers.png` | `cube` | 1000+ Containers Shipped |
| `ontime.png` | `clock` | 98% On-Time Delivery |

The `*-tinted.png` variants are generated by `scripts/tint-icons3d.mjs` — a
navy→cream duotone pass (navy shadows, warm cream/gold highlights) that brings
the neutral clay onto Ekta's palette while preserving the 3D depth. The tinted
variants are what the site ships; originals are kept here for provenance.

Certifications: a guarantee of excellence

Quality products and services, environmental consciousness and good working conditions are our top priorities.

Forest Stewardship Council

FSC forest management certification confirms that the forest is being managed in a way that preserves biological diversity and benefits the lives of local people and workers while ensuring it sustains economic viability.

Program for the Endorsement of Forest Certification

PEFC, the Program for the Endorsement of Forest Certification, is a leading global alliance of national forest certification systems. They work throughout the forest supply chain to promote good practices in the forest and ensure that forest-based products are produced with respect for the highest ecological, social and ethical standards.

Vegetable-based ink: an eco-friendly printing

We are committed to reducing the environmental footprint by using vegetable-based or biodegradable soybean printing inks. By doing so, we aim to minimize the impact on the environment and promote sustainable printing practices.

Facility and Merchandise Authorization

Disney’s Facility and Merchandise Authorization (FAMA) certification confirms that a company may manufacture and distribute products bearing the intellectual property of The Walt Disney Company and its subsidiaries, such as Marvel, Pixar and Star Wars. Companies that receive this certification are committed to respecting human rights, monitoring the safety and integrity of their products and reducing the environmental footprint of their supply chain.

Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit

SMETA (Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit) is one of the world’s leading ethical trade membership organization, working with businesses to improve working conditions in global supply chains.

Idealliance G7

G7 Master status is granted to physical facilities qualified to use the G7 Proof-to-Print Process and uses the most modern technology, techniques, and press controls to produce a close visual match from proof to print.

Ethical Toy Program

The Ethical Toy Program is the ethical supply chain program for the global toy, childhood, entertainment and publishing industry. It monitors, manages and resolves social sustainability issues to ensure the well-being of factory workers.

Global Security Verification

Intertek’s Global Security Verification (GSV) program employs a cutting-edge methodology for promoting best practices throughout the global trade industry, enabling importers and suppliers to mitigate risks associated with the cross-border transport of goods while simultaneously expediting their arrival at destination markets.

ISO 9001:2015

ISO 9001:2015— Quality Management Systems is the international standard that certifies that organizations can consistently deliver products and services that meet customer requirements. It demonstrates the organization’s adherence to applicable legal and regulatory requirements by aiming to increase customer satisfaction through effectively applying the quality system.

ISO 14001:2015

ISO 14001:2015— Environmental Management Systems is the international standard for organizations managing their environmental responsibilities systematically. This standard contributes to the environmental pillar of sustainable development.

BSCI - Business Social Compliance

The Business Social Compliance Initiative (BSCI) aims to protect workers' rights and to improve their working conditions. It guides businesses on the path to a more ethical production, inspired by the International Labour Organization. It ensures the safety and well-being of the workers, with fair working hours and salaries. It forbids all forced and child labour. Finally, it is also committed to protecting the environment.
